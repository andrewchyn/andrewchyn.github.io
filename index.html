About Me

I am currently a 3rd year Engineering Physics student at the University of British Columbia. If you're not sure what that is, I would describe it as a mix between a Mechatronics and an Honours Physics program. My technical strengths lie in software development, but I'm trying to gain more experience in other fields however I can.



I'd love to be able to work in a fusion power, renewable energy, quantum computing, or a machine learning related field, but I'm open to anything cool.
